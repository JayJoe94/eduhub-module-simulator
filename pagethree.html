Why real‑world applications?

Data turns challenges into opportunities. In Ghana, it supports better hospital capacity planning, fairer access to credit, targeted campaigns, resilient farms, and data‑driven public services.

Think‑Pair‑Share: Where do you notice data in daily life (MoMo, hospital records, e‑commerce, GPS)?

Common process (any industry)

  1. Collect (transactions, sensors, surveys, EMRs, census).
  2. Clean (deduplicate, handle missing, standardise).
  3. Analyse (descriptive → predictive → prescriptive).
  4. Interpret (context, bias, ethics).
  5. Act (dashboards, experiments, policies).

Industry Applications (Ghana/Africa)

▸ Healthcare Korle‑Bu, CHAG, DHIMS2, malaria forecasting

Use cases: triage prioritisation, stock forecasting, outbreak detection, referral optimisation.

  • Dataset ideas: DHIMS2 monthly morbidity, EMR visit logs, pharmacy stock & lead times, weather (rainfall).
  • KPIs: wait time, bed occupancy, stock‑out rate, case fatality ratio, referral turnaround.
During rainy seasons, a district hospital sees spikes in fever cases. Which predictor is most useful to forecast malaria admissions?
▸ Finance & Banking MoMo, micro‑loans, credit scoring

Use cases: alternative credit scoring from mobile money, fraud detection, churn prediction, loan default modelling.

  • Dataset ideas: MoMo transaction histories, airtime patterns, KYC, repayment logs.
  • KPIs: approval rate, default rate, NPL, CAC vs LTV, fraud alerts.
A microfinance startup in Accra wants to extend loans to first‑time borrowers. Which data best supports inclusion without traditional credit history?
▸ Marketing & Retail Jumia, Melcom, basket analysis

Use cases: product recommendations, A/B testing promotions, market basket analysis, demand forecasting.

  • Dataset ideas: POS receipts, clickstreams, campaign logs, SKU stock levels.
  • KPIs: conversion rate, average order value, basket size, promo ROI.
If customers frequently buy cooking oil + rice together, which strategy is most effective?
▸ Agriculture Ashanti rainfall, soil, extension services

Use cases: crop yield prediction, weather‑aware planting, fertiliser optimisation, pest alerts via SMS.

  • Dataset ideas: rainfall & temperature, soil pH, satellite NDVI, input prices, extension visit logs.
  • KPIs: yield/acre, post‑harvest loss %, input cost/acre, adoption rate.
Forecast shows 40% less rain this season in Ejura. What’s the best advice for maize farmers?
▸ Public Services & Government GSS, census, resource allocation

Use cases: school & clinic siting, road maintenance prioritisation, targeting social programmes, tax analytics.

  • Dataset ideas: census & PHC, district health & education stats, road condition surveys, geospatial layers.
  • KPIs: service coverage, travel time to facility, enrolment ratio, equity indices.
Census shows a district with a surge of children under 10. What should be prioritised first?

Quick Scenario Practice

Scenario: A clinic faces monthly medicine stock‑outs.

  1. Pull last 18 months of pharmacy issues & receipts; link to outpatient visits.
  2. Seasonality check (month, rainfall); compute moving average demand.
  3. Set reorder point = avg lead time × demand + safety stock.
  4. Track KPIs: stock‑out days/month, forecast error (MAPE), expiries.
Which metric best evaluates the forecast accuracy of medicine demand?
